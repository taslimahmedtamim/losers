The Heart of Everything

The Main Branch is the governing body of the entire Losers community. It oversees all sub-communities, coordinates cross-branch events, manages community-wide decisions, and ensures every member feels connected to the larger family.

All major community events, policy decisions, and new branch approvals go through the Main Branch leadership. It's where the vision and direction of Losers is shaped.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Community governance and policy making
  • Cross-branch event coordination
  • New branch approval and oversight
  • Member welfare and conflict resolution
