OUR ORIGIN

From Humble Beginnings to a Multi-Branch Organization

What started in 2024 as a handful of close friends bonding over casual gaming sessions has since evolved into one of the most active social communities in our circle — with 30 dedicated members and multiple specialized branches.

The name "Losers" was a deliberate, tongue-in-cheek choice. It reflects our core philosophy: winning isn't everything. The real victory lies in the friendships we forge, the memories we share, and the culture of belonging we've cultivated together.

Today, Losers operates across eFootball, Clash of Clans, and social engagement verticals — each branch led by passionate members, each contributing to a larger mission of community, growth, and shared purpose.

Milestones

Our Journey So Far

Key moments that shaped the Losers community into what it is today.

2024

The Beginning

A small group of friends came together with a shared love for gaming and hanging out. The idea of "Losers" was born — a community where everyone wins through friendship.

Mid 2024

First Branch Established

The eFootball Community became our first official branch, bringing organized tournaments and competitive gaming to the heart of Losers.

Late 2024

COC Clan Goes Live

Our Clash of Clans branch launched, rapidly growing into one of the most active clans in the community with dedicated war strategies and clan war leagues.

2025

30 Members Milestone

Losers crossed the 30-member mark, established a formal leadership structure, and organized 30+ community events throughout the year.

2026

Expanding Horizons

With new branches in the pipeline and a growing presence, Losers continues to evolve — staying true to its roots while reaching for new heights.
