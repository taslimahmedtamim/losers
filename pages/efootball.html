ABOUT THE BRANCH

The Virtual Football Arena

The Losers eFootball Community is one of our most active and competitive branches. Founded for passionate eFootball (PES) enthusiasts, this branch brings together players of all skill levels for an amazing gaming experience.

Whether you're a seasoned pro or just picking up the controller, you'll find a welcoming community that loves the beautiful game. We organize regular tournaments, friendly matches, and skill-building sessions to help everyone improve.

What We Do

Regular activities and events within the eFootball Community.

🏆

Monthly Tournaments

Competitive tournaments every month with brackets, seedings, and prizes for top performers.

🎮

Weekly Friendly Matches

Casual gaming sessions every week where members can play, practice, and have fun together.

📊

Seasonal League

A season-long league system where players accumulate points and climb the community ladder.

📚

Skill Building Sessions

Strategy sharing, formation discussions, and tips from top players to help everyone improve.

🌍

Inter-Community Matches

Friendly matches and tournaments against other eFootball communities to test our skills.

📺

Live Streaming

Big matches and tournaments are streamed live for community members to watch and enjoy.
